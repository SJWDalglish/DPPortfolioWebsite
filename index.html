My Story...

1 / 8

During school I discovered a passion for the Earth, science and music

2 / 8

Born and raised in Hill Head, South England, I was the first in my family to go to college, graduating from Plymouth University with a Bachelors of Science in Geology

During university, my music got a small following. After graduation I was scouted by an agency. This allowed me to travel the world performing and writing.

I moved back to England, setting up my own music business. This is when I started to play around with web development.

Due to lockdowns, suddenly, live music was not allowed in England. Looking for a job, I was lucky enough to know a few businesses, who hired me as a freelance developer.

In late 2021, I moved to Washington DC, officially becoming an American resident! I graduated TrueCoders bootcamp in 2022, cementing all I had taught myself over the previous few years and discovering much more.

I'm currently living in Alexandria, Virginia, enthusiastically looking for work as a Software Developer, excited to make a difference at a great company.
